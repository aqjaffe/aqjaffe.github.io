I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Department of Statistics at Columbia University, working under the supervision of Bodhisattva Sen. Previously, I received my PhD from UC Berkeley and my BS from Stanford.

My research is at the intersection of probability, statistics, and geometry. Some specific topics I'm interested in include: clustering, variational inference, empirical Bayes, non-Euclidean statistics, Riemannian optimization, stochastic optimization, and optimal transport.
